Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.
2. Do not scan unknown QR codes, especially to “receive” money.
3. Always verify payment requests before approving them.
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links from unknown sources.
5. Use only official apps from trusted app stores.
6. Do not install screen-sharing apps when asked by strangers.
7. Enable app locks and two-factor authentication on payment apps.
8. Regularly check your bank statements for unusual transactions.

Financial/UPI Safety Tips:
9. Remember: Money is received without entering UPI PIN — PIN is only needed to send money.
10. Double-check the receiver’s name before making any payment.

Report Fraud:
If you become a victim, immediately report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Or call the helpline: 1930
